## Overview

In partnership with Raven Studio, we developed a secure, conversion-optimized, and SEO-centric web presence for **Greyman Sienna**, a creative agency expanding its digital footprint. The primary objectives: bulletproof reliability, a seamless user journey, and end-to-end SEO automation to boost discoverability and performance.

## Security and Reliability

> “If users can’t trust your platform, they won’t use it — no matter how beautiful it is.”  
> — Black Cap, Lead Architect

We designed the system architecture with **24/7 security, stability, and scalability** in mind.

### Key Implementations

- **Data Encryption**: All data is transmitted over TLS 1.3 and stored using AES-256 encryption standards.
- **Active Threat Detection**: Real-time monitoring with event-based alerting to mitigate intrusion risks.
- **Access Control**: Role-based permission layers to protect backend interfaces and internal dashboards.
- **Uptime Assurance**: Redundant systems and failover infrastructure to maintain service during traffic spikes.

## UX & Conversion Optimization

Led by **Black Hoodie**, the UX strategy focused on eliminating friction points and optimizing decision flows to drive meaningful engagement.

### Key Improvements

- **Drop-Off Analysis**: Heatmaps and session recordings identified high-friction areas in the purchase funnel.
- **CTA Optimization**: Refactored call-to-action language and placements for higher engagement.
- **Navigation Hierarchy**: Improved information architecture for shorter user paths to goal completion.
- **Micro-Interactions**: Subtle animations and confirmations that guided user behavior without distraction.

### Tools Used

- Hotjar (user behavior analytics)
- Google Optimize (A/B testing)
- Figma (UI/UX prototyping)

## Agile Delivery

> “We don’t just deliver fast. We deliver with precision.”  
> — Black Truck, PM

Structured in two-week sprints, the project ran on an agile framework with transparency and speed.

### Collaboration Stack

- **Slack**: Daily standups and async check-ins
- **Trello & Notion**: Kanban-based task tracking and documentation
- **Zero Downtime Deployment**: QA-backed release with live monitoring and rollback protocols

## SEO Automation

From metadata structuring to automated sitemaps, we implemented systems that handle the heavy lifting of SEO behind the scenes.

### SEO Features

- **Dynamic Meta Tags**: Auto-generated from CMS input using templated logic
- **Schema.org Integration**: Rich data snippets for improved SERP visibility
- **Canonical & Open Graph Tags**: Ensured uniformity and social preview optimization
- **Sitemap Automation**: Cron-based sitemap generation and pinging of search engines

## Outcome

The redesigned platform has not only improved security and performance but also resulted in a **38% uplift in conversion rates** and a **65% improvement in page indexation speed**.

- **Bounce Rate**: ↓ 27%  
- **Average Time on Site**: ↑ 42%  
- **Search Traffic**: ↑ 55% in 60 days post-launch
